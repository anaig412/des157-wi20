Topic Brainstorm Summary

When I need to brainstorm, I tend to use associative word lists. I have tried mapping, but sometimes, not all the words that come to mind are related to the one before it. Sometimes I just generate random new ideas, and a word list gives me the freedom to list off those ideas without being concerned about how they relate.

While looking through our affinity mapping exercise, I was drawn to the categories concerning identity, diversity, gender, and LGBT-QIA. As a result, I put these at the top of my list, and began coming up with new ideas. Ultimately, I am envisioning an interactive project that provides a platform for youth to be able to learn more about different types of idenitities (culture, gender, etc.). This can happen through Q&A, quizzes, articles, storytelling, and various other resources. Although I would like to go in this direction, I do not have a concrete idea yet.
